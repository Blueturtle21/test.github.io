The Marketing Tech
Behind This Site

March 7, 2018


One of the things I wanted to do when I created this site was to experiment with the new and exciting digital marketing technologies and put in to practice some of the strategies I learned in my marketing classes.

I focused on a few key areas that would help me convert leads and improve the overall user experience. These areas included in-app messaging, page speed insights, google Analytics, and search engine optimization.

In-App Messaging

I installed Drift, an in-app messaging service, to talk to people visiting my site in real time. Want to see it in action? Click on the Drift icon in the bottom right corner and say hello!

PageSpeed Insights

In order to make my site faster and more mobile-friendly, I used Google PageSpeed Insights to make the following imrovements.

  • Optimized images
  • Prioritize above the fold content
  • Eliminated render-blocking CSS and Javascript

Google Analytics

I installed Google Analytics as a way to measure my site's performance and track potential conversions.

Search Engine Optimization

I wanted my site to appear first when people search for my name, so I took the following steps to improve my site's search engine ranking:

  • Started an AdWords campaign
  • Opened new social media accounts
  • Linked social media accounts to my website
